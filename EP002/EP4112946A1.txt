abstract
    Hydraulic arrangement (1) for a work vehicle provided with a boom configured to be controlled via a pair of cylinders (4, 5) and an energy recovery system (7) comprising a pair of support cylinders (8, 9) configured to cooperate with the pair of cylinders (4, 5) and at least an accumulator (11),the hydraulic arrangement (1) comprising a feeding conduit (12) fluidly connecting the accumulator (11) and the support cylinders (8, 9) and a selection module (31) fluidly interposed on the feeding conduit (12), the selection module (31) being configured to regulate the fluid passage between accumulator (11), support cylinders (8, 9) and a tank (20, 20') of the work vehicle.
