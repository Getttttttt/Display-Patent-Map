abstract
    A heat exchanger (10) includes a plurality of partition sheets (15) and frames (25, 55) that are alternately stacked. A frame portion (30, 60) of each of the frames (25, 55) has a ridge (34, 64) and an elongated recess (35, 65). The ridge (34, 64) of one of an adjacent pair of the frames (25, 55) of the heat exchanger (10) fits into the elongated recess (35, 65) of the other. The partition sheets (15) are each sandwiched between the ridge (34, 64) of one of a pair of the first frame (25) and the second frame (55) adjacent to the partition sheet (15) and the elongated recess (35, 65) of the other.
