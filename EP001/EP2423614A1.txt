abstract
    An air handling unit (2) uses at least one or more plate heat exchangers (28, 30) which receive their cooling air supplies in parallel from a low level fresh air supply which can be adiabatically cooled to maximise efficiency. The recirculated air from sealed ductwork at a high level in a data room is cooled by the plate heat exchangers in series.
