abstract
    An air conditioning system for conditioning room air comprising an air supply device (1) having an extraction air chamber (2), into which room extraction air from the room is introduced, and a power source (3) for powering the air supply de-vice. The air supply device comprises at least one peltier-element (4) having a first side (20) arranged to condition at least part of the room extraction air before it being diffused into the room.
