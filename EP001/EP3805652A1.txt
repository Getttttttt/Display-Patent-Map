abstract
    A heat exchange ventilator (100) includes an air supply blower that creates a supply air flow, an air exhaust blower that creates an exhaust air flow, a heat exchanger that performs heat exchange between the supply air flow and the exhaust air flow, a housing (1) that houses the air supply blower, the air exhaust blower, and the heat exchanger, a first drainage outlet (25) that is at a lower portion of the housing (1) to cause drain water collected in the housing (1) to drain out of the housing when the housing (1) assumes a first posture to have the air supply blower, the heat exchanger, and the air exhaust blower in a horizontal line, and a second drainage outlet (26) that is at a lower portion of the housing (1) to cause drain water held in the housing (1) to drain out of the housing when the housing (1) assumes a second posture to have the air supply blower, the heat exchanger, and the air exhaust blower in a vertical line.
