abstract
    An air supply device (1) for extracting exhaust air from a room and leading fresh supply air into the room comprising a casing (2) and a body, made of elastic material, arranged inside the casing (2). The body is divided in at least an upper part (3) and a lower part (4) which are arranged on top of each other. The upper part (3) comprises an internal part (9) and a frame (10), wherein the internal part (9) is arranged inside the frame (10). The internal part (9) is arranged to be installed inside the frame (10) in a first position or in a second position, wherein in the second position the internal part (9) has been rotated 180Â° in horizontal plane compared to the first position.
