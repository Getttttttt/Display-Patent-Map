abstract
    External heat exchanger for improving the efficiency of a heating system comprising a heater arranged for heating up heating water in a closed heating water circuit, comprising a tank connectable to the closed heating water circuit and comprising an input opening for receiving heating water and an output opening for outputting heating water. The external heat exchanger further comprises means for exchanging heat from exhaust gasses from a heater to heating water in the tank.
