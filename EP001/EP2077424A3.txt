abstract
    A dehumidifier unit (1, 20) comprising an inlet aperture (2) for the air to be dehumidified, an outlet aperture (3) for the dehumidified air, a fan (4) to draw in the air from an environment (A) and guarantee the passage of the air through the apertures (2, 3), a dehumidifying element (5, 22) connected to a hydraulic circuit (6), and two heat exchangers (8, 9) arranged respectively upstream and downstream of the dehumidifying element (5, 22) with respect to the direction of the airflow. The heat exchangers (8, 9) are connected to one another by means of a separate hydraulic circuit (10) comprising a pump (11).
