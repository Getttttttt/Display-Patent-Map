abstract
    A system includes a syngas cooler (18) configured to cool a syngas (16). The syngas cooler (18) includes a first syngas cooler section (20) configured to cool the syngas (16) and a second syngas cooler section (30) configured to cool the syngas (16) and generate a first superheated steam (32) with an enthalpy of less than approximately 3800 kJ/kg.
