abstract
    The present invention refers to a heat pump apparatus for air renewal in rooms and, in particular, to an improved compact heat pump apparatus with components contained in a single casing to be mounted inside a room. Moreover, the invention is related to a method of operation of said apparatus inside a room able to activate a heat recovery action and/or renewal of stale air, heating, cooling or dehumidification.
