abstract
    A hydraulic system that can implement effective practical use of energy is provided. A hydraulic system according to the present invention has a hydraulic pump 1 driven by a hydraulic pump motor 3, a loading cylinder 13 and a power steering cylinder 15 which are driven by pressure oil supplied from the hydraulic pump 1, and power supply means for supplying power to the hydraulic pump motor 3. The power supply means comprises a stack 19 of a fuel cell, a fuel supply passage 59 for supplying hydrogen gas to the stack 19, fuel circulating passages 61, 63 in which a hydrogen pump 55 for joining unreacted hydrogen gas discharged from the stack 19 into the fuel supply passage 59 to circulate hydrogen gas is disposed, and a regenerator 57 which is driven by pressure oil to generate rotational force. The hydrogen pump 55 is rotationally driven by the regenerator 57.
