abstract
    An air-conditioning unit (100) is described. The air-conditioning unit (100) is suitable for overhead installation in, for example, a suspended ceiling. The air-conditioning unit (100) is configured to supply conditioned air from a front side (110), and to expel exhaust air from a rear side (120).
