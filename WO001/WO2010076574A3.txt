abstract
    In a first aspect of the present invention there is provided a heat exchange module, the module comprising: a first fluid inlet and a first fluid outlet, the first fluid inlet and first fluid outlet being provided in fluid communication with one another by means of a plurality of tube elements of the module; and a second fluid inlet and a second fluid outlet, the second fluid inlet and second fluid outlet being provided in fluid communication with one another, wherein the module is arranged whereby an outer surface of each of the plurality of tube elements is exposed to fluid flowing between the second fluid inlet and second fluid outlet whereby heat may be exchanged between respective fluids flowing between the respective fluid inlets and fluid outlets.
