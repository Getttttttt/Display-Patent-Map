abstract
    A hydraulic control system (50) for a machine (10) is disclosed. The hydraulic control system may have a pump (58) configured to pressurize fluid, and a motor (49) driven by a flow of pressurized fluid from the pump. The hydraulic control system may also have a first accumulator (108) configured to receive pressurized fluid discharged from the motor and to selectively supply pressurized fluid to the motor, and a second accumulator (110) configured to receive pressurized fluid discharged from the motor and selectively supply pressurized fluid to the motor.
