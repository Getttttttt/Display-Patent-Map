abstract
    System (100) for producing thermal energy comprising: - at least one heating network (2); - at least one boiler (5) connected with the said network (2) by means of at least one duct (3) for the delivery of a thermal fluid to the said network (2) and at least one duct (4) for the return the thermal fluid from the said network (2); said condensing boiler (5) comprising at least one combustion chamber fed with a fuel comprising hydrogen; - at least one duct (7) for discharging the flue gas coming from the said condensing boiler (5); characterized by comprising - at least one condenser (14) in which the flue gas is made to flow; - at least one unit (9) for heating the temperature of the thermal fluid at the return duct (4) for increasing the temperature of the thermal fluid entering the boiler.
