abstract
    A heat exchanger for use in an indirect fired heater, said heat exchanger comprising an arcuate process airflow pattern. The heat exchanger is a multi-pass heat exchanger for improving heat exchanger efficiencies. The heat exchanger comprises a final pass section adjacent a process flow inlet and a primary drum situated adjacent a process airflow outlet.
