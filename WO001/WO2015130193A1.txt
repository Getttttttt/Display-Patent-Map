abstract
    ﻿The invention relates to systems supplying ship consumers with gas fuel on carriers transporting LNG and using as main energy source the transported natural gas in liquefied state and, in particular, effective usage for these purposes of continuously generated gas due to natural evaporating of LNG in cargo tanks. The way is based on the continuous usage first of all of gas, generated in cargo tanks as the main energy source for power installations of an LNG carrier, compression of excess gas by the compressor, that can take place at low speeds of movement of a vessel or standing, its accumulation in high pressure tanks (200 bar and more) and further usage, when the consumption of gas exceeds its generation in cargo tanks.
