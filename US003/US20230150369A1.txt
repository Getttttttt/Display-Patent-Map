abstract
    Provided is a vehicle that, in order to promote the safety of a user having an active implantable medical device implanted therein, is configured to acquire a strength of a magnetic field inside the vehicle, notify the user of the strength of the magnetic field, and control at least one electric device among a plurality of electric devices in the vehicle, in order to reduce the strength of the magnetic field. The vehicle may be a combustion engine vehicle and/or an electric vehicle.
