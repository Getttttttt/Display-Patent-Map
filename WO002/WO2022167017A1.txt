abstract
    The present invention provides a liquid composition for extracting nucleic acid(s) from pathogens, said liquid composition containing 0.01-0.1 vol.% of at least one ionic detergent, 1-4 vol.% of at least one non-ionic detergent, and Na3PO4 and/or NaHCO3. Furthermore, methods of using this liquid composition are provided.
