abstract
    There is disclosed an energy management system for a towed vehicle comprising; an energy harvesting system for harvesting electrical energy from the energy of the towed vehicle; an energy storage system for storing said harvested electrical energy; and a computer controller for controlling the release of said stored electrical energy to one or more components of the towed vehicle for operating the towed vehicle.
