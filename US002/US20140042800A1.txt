abstract
    There is provided a brake system for performing regenerative braking and friction braking to brake a rotating body, the brake system including: a manipulation part configured to provide a brake input; a regenerative braking unit configured to perform regenerative braking; a friction braking unit configured to perform friction braking; and a control unit configured to control the brake system, wherein the friction braking unit includes: a friction working part configured to brake a rotating body by friction; a pneumatic line system configured to operate the friction working part; a first valve part connected to the pneumatic line system; and a second valve part connected to the pneumatic line system and connected in parallel to the first valve part.
