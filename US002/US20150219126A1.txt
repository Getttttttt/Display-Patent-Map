abstract
    There is provided a multi-function hydraulic hybrid swing circuit including a fluid source, swing circuit and swing supply circuit. The swing supply circuit includes at least one accumulator and a pump/motor operatively connected to a power source. The pump/motor is fluidly connected to the swing circuit, the accumulator, and the fluid source. The pump/motor is configured to provide both closed loop and open loop operation with the swing circuit.
