abstract
    A container-type compressed air storage power generation device (2) comprises compressors (5 a-5 c); a tank (8); power generators (9 a-9 c); a control device (12); and a container (4). The compressors (5 a-5 c) compress air. The tank (8) is driven by air supplied from the compressors (5 a-5 c). The power generators (9 a-9 c) are driven by air supplied from the tank (8). The control device drives and controls the compressors (5 a-5 c) and the power generators (9 a-9 c). The container (4) houses the compressors (5 a-5 c) and the power generators (9 a-9 c), and the tank (8) is disposed outside the container (4). Therefore, the container-type compressed air storage power generation device (2) is easy to transport and construct on-site.
